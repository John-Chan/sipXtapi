Setting Up Email

For inbox owners to receive email notification of their voicemail messages, you specify up to three POP3 mail servers on which to such email notification***. Inbox owners define the specific email addresses where they wish to receive notification of new voicemail messages.

Add a Mail Server

To add a mail server:

  1. Click the Set Up Email link in the left column.

  2. Click Add Mail Server.

  3. Enter the mail account information.

  4. Click Save.

Other options

Edit a Mail Server

To edit an email server:

  1. Click the Set Up Email link in the left column.

  2. Click in the Edit column of the account you want.

  3. Clear the information you want to change.

  4. Enter the new  mail account information.

  5. Click Save.

Other options

Delete a Mail Server

To delete a mail server:

  1. Click the Set Up Email link in the left column.

  2. Click in the Delete column of the account you want to remove.
