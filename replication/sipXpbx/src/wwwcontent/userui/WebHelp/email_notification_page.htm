Email Notification

This is the Email Notification page. You can set up the SipXpbx Voicemail to notify you by email when you have a new voice mail message on this page.

 

Remember: SipXpbx Voicemail can send you email notification on POP3 email accounts only. You can receive notification on as many as three email accounts.

 

The email notification you receive contains a .wav file copy of the new voice mail message. If your PC is enabled to play sound, you can launch the voice mail message from the email.

Start receiving email notification

To start receiving email notification:

  • Click the Email Notification link in the left column.

  • Click the Add Mail Server link.

  • Enter the following information for the email account:

      • Mail Server

      • Mail Server User Name

      • Mail Server Password

      • Mail Server Port

  • Click Save.

Stop receiving email notification

To stop receiving email notification

  • Click the Email Notification link in the left column.

  • Click in the Delete column of the email account you want.
