Welcome

With SIPxchange, you can:

  • Listen to and maintain your voicemail messages

  • Create personal folders

  • Listen to and choose the voicemail greeting to play to your callers

  • Get notification of new voicemail messages at an email address

For help accessing voicemail messages and features from a phone, print this quick reference guide.

Tip: For best results, use Internet Explorer version 6.00 or above, or Netscape version 6.2 or above, to access SIPxchange. Your browser should not cache pages. For example:

  • For Internet Explorer 6.0, from the Tools menu select Internet Options. On the Advanced tab, under Temporary Internet files click Settings. Under the Check for newer versions of stored pages option, choose the Every visit to the page option.

  • For Netscape 7.0, from the Edit menu select Preferences, click on Advanced, then Cache, and under Compare the page in the cache to the page on the network, choose the Every time I view the page option.

Please contact your System Administrator for further assistance with the SIPxchange phone system.
