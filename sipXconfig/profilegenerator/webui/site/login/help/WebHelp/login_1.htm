Log in

Both end users and administrators can log in to SIPxchange. SIPxchange offers different options based on the user name you supply when you log in.

End users

To log in as an end user, you enter your:

  • User ID or extension number.

  • Personal identification number (PIN).

Then click Submit.

Administrators

To log in as an administrator, you enter:

  • The name superadmin.

  • Your organization's password.

Then click Submit.
