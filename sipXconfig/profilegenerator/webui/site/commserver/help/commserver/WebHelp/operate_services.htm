Start a service

You can only start a service that is not running (with a status of "Stopped"). To start a service:

  1. Click Operations in the left column.
  2. Find the name of the service you want to start.
  3. Click the button in the Start column for that service.
    The selected service and any subsidiary services start.

Other options

Stop a service

You can only stop a service that is currently running. To stop a service:

  1. Click Operations in the left column.
  2. Find the name of the service you want to stop.
  3. Click the button in the Stop column for that service.
    The selected service and any subsidiary services stop.

Other options

Restart a service

You can only restart a service that is currently running. A restart stops the service, then starts it again. To restart a service:

  1. Click Operations in the left column.
  2. Find the name of the service you want to restart.
  3. Click the button in the Restart column for that service.
    The selected service and any subsidiary services stop and then restart.

Other options

Refresh the display

You can update the data that displays on this page manually or automatically.

To update manually, click the Refresh button at the top of the page, just above the "Operate Services" heading.

To refresh the display automatically:

  1. Select a time frame from the drop down list at the top of the page.

  2. Click Apply.

Other options

Review details

To review details for a service:

  1. Click Operations in the left column.
  2. Click the name of the service you want to review. A new page displays information that includes:
    • Group
    • Status
    • Host
    • PIDs
    • Up time
    • Dependencies
    • Dependents
  3. To return to the services list, click Operations.
