Configure Services

This page lists the system-wide SIPxchange services that you configure. To configure a service:

  1. Click Configuration in the left column.

  2. Click the name of the service you want to configure. A new page displays the settings and options that apply to that service. You can configure:

    • Global Definitions

    • Gateways

    • Dial Plans

    • SipAuthProxy

    • SipProxy

    • SipRegistrar

    • SipStatus

After you finish changing system-wide settings, be sure to restart the affected service.
