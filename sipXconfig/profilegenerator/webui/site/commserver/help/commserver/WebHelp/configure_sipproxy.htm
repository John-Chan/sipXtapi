Configure SipProxy

SipProxy is a forking SIP Proxy server that routes SIP requests and responses that it receives from your SIPxchange-managed phones, SIP gateways, and other SIP entities. You configure settings for SipProxy on this page.

To configure SipProxy:

  1. Click Configuration in the left column.
  2. Find SipProxy on the list of services.
  3. Click the icon in the Configure column for SipProxy. A new page lists current values.
  4. You define configuration settings by making changes directly in its text box.
    To learn more about a particular setting, click the help icon   to its right.
  5. Click Save when you complete your edits.

Your new settings do not take effect until you restart SipProxy.
