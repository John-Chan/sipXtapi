Additional Settings

If directed to do so by Pingtel's Technical Assistance Center, you use the text box on this page to enter settings that do not display in other categories.

To enter an additional setting, you enter the parameter name as it appears in the phone’s pinger-config file, a space, a colon (:), another space, and then the value. For example:

PHONESET_HTTP_PORT : 80
