Manage Call Handling

On this page, you define how your Pingtel phone handles incoming calls. The settings on this page interact to enable or disable the following features:

  • Do Not Disturb

  • Call Waiting

  • Call Forwarding

  • Incoming Call Alert

Do Not Disturb

Setting your phone to Do Not Disturb prevents your phone from alerting you to any incoming calls.

  • To turn this feature on, set Do Not Disturb to ENABLE. As a reminder, this icon displays on your phone's home screen: .

If you have a voicemail inbox, SIPxchange forwards all callers to your inbox. If you do not have a voicemail inbox, SIPxchange forwards callers to your forward on no answer or forward on busy address.

  • To turn this feature off, set Do Not Disturb to DISABLE.

When all changes on this page are complete, click Save. Your phone restarts.

Tip: If you use an instant xpressa softphone, you may need to enter your PIN on the phone top for new settings to become active. Your system administrator controls this security setting.

Call Waiting

The call waiting feature alerts you to calls that come in while you are on another call by playing a tone.

  • To turn call waiting on, set If a call is in progress, play call waiting alert or act as busy to ALERT. If you Ignore a waiting call, SIPxchange redirects the call to voicemail.

  • To turn call waiting off, set If a call is in progress, play call waiting alert or act as busy to BUSY. If you have voicemail, SIPxchange redirects calls that come in when your phone is busy to voicemail. If you do not, your phone plays a busy signal to your callers.

When all changes on this page are complete, click Save. Your phone restarts.

Tip: If you use an instant xpressa softphone, you may need to enter your PIN on the phone top for new settings to become active. Your system administrator controls this security setting.

Call Forwarding

You can set your phone to redirect incoming calls:

  • When you don’t answer

  • When your phone is busy

  • All the time

You define the destination for your calls. You can provide the same destination or a different one for each situation.

To forward on no answer

  • Set If no answer, phone should to FORWARD_ON_NO_ANSWER. Additional fields display.

  • Define how long you want your phone to ring by entering a number of seconds in the Forward on no answer within (seconds) field. Each ring takes approximately 6 seconds, so you should supply 12 or 18 for this setting.

  • Enter a destination address in the Forward on no answer to field. The address can be a phone number or SIP URL.

Tip: To forward calls to a phone outside your SIPxchange system, such as another office location, a cell phone, or your home phone, enter the number beginning with its area code, without any leading digits. For example, enter 7819385306, rather than 917819385306.

To cancel this feature, set If no answer, phone should to RING.

When all changes on this page are complete, click Save. Your phone restarts.

Tip: If you use an instant xpressa softphone, you may need to enter your PIN on the phone top for new settings to become active. Your system administrator controls this security setting.

To forward when busy

  • Make sure that call waiting is not active: set If a call is in progress, play call waiting alert or act as busy to BUSY.

  • Set When busy, forward calls or play busy signal to FORWARD. An additional field displays.

  • Enter a destination address in the Forward if busy to field. The address can be a phone number or SIP URL.

Tip: To forward calls to a phone outside your SIPxchange system, such as another office location, a cell phone, or your home phone, enter the number beginning with its area code, without any leading digits. For example, enter 7819385306, rather than 917819385306.

To cancel this feature, either set When busy, forward calls or play busy signal to BUSY (to play a busy signal instead of forwarding) or make call waiting active.

When all changes on this page are complete, click Save. Your phone restarts.

Tip: If you use an instant xpressa softphone, you may need to enter your PIN on the phone top for new settings to become active. Your system administrator controls this security setting.

To forward all calls

  • Set Forward all calls to ENABLE. Additional fields display.

  • Enter a destination address in the Forward all calls to field. This address can be a phone number or SIP URL.

As a reminder, this icon displays on your phone's home screen: .

Tip: To forward calls to a phone outside your SIPxchange system, such as another office location, a cell phone, or your home phone, enter the number beginning with its area code, without any leading digits. For example, enter 7819385306, rather than 917819385306.

To cancel this feature, set Forward all calls to DISABLE.

When all changes on this page are complete, click Save. Your phone restarts.

Tip: If you use an instant xpressa softphone, you may need to enter your PIN on the phone top for new settings to become active. Your system administrator controls this security setting.

Other options

Incoming Call Alert

You choose how your phone indicates that you have an incoming call: by playing a ring tone, flashing the message light, or both.

  • To play the ring tone only, set Incoming call alert to AUDIBLE.

  • To flash the message light only, set Incoming call alert to VISUAL.

  • To receive both alerts, set Incoming call alert to BOTH.

When all changes on this page are complete, click Save. Your phone restarts.

Tip: If you use an instant xpressa softphone, you may need to enter your PIN on the phone top for new settings to become active. Your system administrator controls this security setting.
