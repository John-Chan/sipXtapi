Change PIN

You change your Personal Identification Number (PIN) on this page.

To change your PIN:

  • Click Change PIN in the left column.

  • Enter your old PIN.

  • Enter your new PIN.

  • Confirm the new PIN by entering it a second time.

  • Click Save. Your phone restarts, and you are automatically logged out.

To log back in to SIPxchange, use your user ID or extension number and your new PIN.
