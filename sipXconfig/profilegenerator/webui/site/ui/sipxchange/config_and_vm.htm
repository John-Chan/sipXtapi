Welcome

Pingtel's SIPxchange system gives you a desktop interface for working with your phone. You use this interface to perform tasks that are easier to do on a PC than on a phone.

You can:

  • Manage Speed Dial Numbers

  • Manage Call Handling Settings

  • Manage Applications

  • Change your PIN

If your system administrator has set up SIPxchange voicemail for you, you also have access to your voicemail. You can:

  • Listen to your voicemail messages

  • Create personal folders

  • Choose the voicemail greeting to play to your callers

For help performing any of these tasks, click the Help link at the top of any Web page presented by the Configuration Server.
