Manage Speed Dial Numbers

On this page, you work with speed dial numbers for your Pingtel phone:

  • Add a speed dial number

  • Edit a speed dial number

  • Delete a speed dial number

Add a speed dial number

To add a speed dial number:

  • Click the add icon  . A popup window opens.

  • Enter four values:

    • The Speed dial number. This is the number you'll dial in place of the full phone number or SIP URL.

    • An identifying Name.

    • The type of address you'll be entering: choose either a Phone number or a SIP URL.

    • The Address: the full phone number or SIP URL.

Be sure to enter phone numbers with all digits required for normal dialing.  For example, if you would normally dial 9 for an outside line, enter 9 followed by the number.

In a phone number, you can include formatting characters such as dashes or spaces, and the special characters p (to indicate a pause) or i (to indicate an interactive wait). These special characters can be useful if the number is for a voicemail inbox, calling card, or other system that uses touch tones.

  • Click Submit.

When all of your changes are complete, click Save. Your phone restarts.

Tip: If you use an instant xpressa softphone, you may need to enter your PIN on the phone top for new settings to become active. Your system administrator controls this security setting.

Edit a speed dial number

To edit a speed dial number:

  • Scroll to find the speed dial number you want to change.

  • Click the edit icon  . A popup window opens.

  • Make your edits.

  • Click Submit.

When all of your changes are complete, click Save. Your phone restarts.

Tip: If you use an instant xpressa softphone, you may need to enter your PIN on the phone top for new settings to become active. Your system administrator controls this security setting.

Delete a speed dial number

To delete a speed dial number:

  • Scroll to find the speed dial number you want to delete.

  • Click the delete icon  .

When all of your changes are complete, click Save. Your phone restarts.

Tip: If you use an instant xpressa softphone, you may need to enter your PIN on the phone top for changes to become active. Your system administrator controls this security setting.
