Linking Email

This is the Link Email page. You can set up your account to notify you by email of new voicemail messages. You can link to voicemail messages on any web-enabled device with sound.

 

Remember: You can receive email notification on as many as three POP3 email accounts.

Start receiving email notification

To start receiving email notification:

  • Click the Link Email link in the left column.

  • Click Add Email.

  • Enter the information for the email account.

  • Click Go.

Stop receiving email notification

To stop receiving email notification:

  • Click the Link Email link in the left column.

  • Click in the Delete column of the email account you want to remove.
