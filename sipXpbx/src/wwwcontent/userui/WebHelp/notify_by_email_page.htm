Add a notification address

To start receiving email notifications when you get new voicemail messages:

  • Click the Manage Notifications link in the left column.

  • Click Add Notification.

  • Enter the email address.

  • To receive each voicemail message as an email attachment, check the Attach voice message check box.

  • Click Save.

Other options

Edit a notification address

To edit an email address:

  • Click the Manage Notifications link in the left column.

  • Click in the Edit column of the address you want to change.

  • Enter the new email address.

  • Check or clear the Attach voice message check box.

  • Click Save.

Other options

Delete a notification address

To delete an email address:

  • Click the Manage notifications link in the left column.

  • Click in the Delete column of the address you want to remove.
