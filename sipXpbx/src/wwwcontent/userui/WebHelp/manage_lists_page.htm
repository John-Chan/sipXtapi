Managing Distribution Lists

This is the Manage Distribution Lists page. You can create, edit, and delete personal distribution lists from this page. You can also add and delete individuals from your personal distribution lists.

 

Remember: Some distribution lists are created by the System Administrator. You can click to view members of a system distribution list. You can only change and delete your own personal distribution lists.

Create a distribution list

To create a distribution list:

  • Click the Manage Distribution Lists link in the left column.

  • Click Add List.

  • Enter the name of your new list.

  • Select the members you want on your list and click the arrows to move them.

Remember: To select multiple addresses, hold down the CTRL key and select.

  • Click Save.

Delete a distribution list

To delete a distribution list:

  • Click the Manage Distribution Lists link in the left column.

  • Click in the Delete column of the list you want to remove.

Remember: Some distribution lists are created by the System Administrator. You can only change and delete your own personal distribution lists.

Rename a distribution list

To rename a distribution list:

  • Click the Manage Distribution Lists link in the left column.

  • Click in the Edit column of the list you want to rename.

Remember: Some distribution lists are created by the System Administrator. You can only change and delete your own personal distribution lists.

  • Clear the current name of the list.

  • Enter the new name of the list.

  • Click Save.
