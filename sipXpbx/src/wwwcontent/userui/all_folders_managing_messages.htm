Working with messages

On this page, you play and delete your messages or move them to other folders.

  • New voicemail messages appear in your Inbox in bold font.

  • After you listen to a new message, it displays in normal font.

In addition to the supplied Inbox, Saved, and Deleted folders, you can add personal folders.

For help accessing voicemail messages and features from a phone, print this quick reference guide.

Play a message

To play a message on this PC:

  • Under the Voicemail Folders menu at the left of the page, click Inbox, Saved, Deleted, or Personal to access the messages in that folder.

  • Click   in the Play column for the message you want to play. The PC's default media player starts and the message plays.

Tip: The PC must be enabled for sound, with the speakers turned on or a headset plugged in, for the message to play.

Move a message to another folder

To move a message from one folder to another:

  • Under the Voicemail Folders menu at the left of the page, click Inbox, Saved, Deleted, or Personal to access the messages in that folder.

  • Click to check the check box next to the message you want to move.

  • In the Move to Folder drop-down list, select the destination folder for the message.

  • Click Move.

The message moves to the selected folder.

Tip: You can create personal folders to help organize your messages.

Other options

Edit the subject of a message

All messages have a subject of "Voice Message" by default. To help you identify and organize messages, you can change this subject to a more meaningful description. To edit the subject of a message:

  • Under the Voicemail Folders menu at the left of the page, click Inbox, Saved, Deleted, or Personal to access the messages in that folder.

  • Click in the Edit column of the message you want to edit.

  • Change the subject text as needed.

  • Click Save.

Delete a message

To delete a message:

  • Under the Voicemail Folders menu at the left of the page, click Inbox, Saved, or Personal to access the messages in that folder.

  • Click to check the check box next to the message you want to delete.

  • Click Delete.

The message moves from its current folder to the Deleted folder.

To permanently remove messages that are in the Deleted folder:

  • Under the Voicemail Folders menu at the left of the page, click Deleted.

  • Click to check the check box next to the message you want to permanently remove.

  • Click Delete.

Alternatively, click Empty Folder in the Deleted folder to permanently remove all of the messages.
